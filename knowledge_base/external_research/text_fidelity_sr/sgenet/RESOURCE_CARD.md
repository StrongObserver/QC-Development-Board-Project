# SGENet Resource Card

## Why It Is Here

SGENet is included as a lightweight semantic-guidance reference for scene text
image super-resolution. It is relevant when RB5 `text_signage` cases need more
than pixel-level sharpness metrics.

## Use When

- exploring OCR/semantic guidance for text SR
- comparing lightweight text SR ideas against TPGSR
- defining future text-specific quality gates

## Do Not Misuse

- Do not claim code is available here.
- Do not prioritize this over the existing RB5 QNN path.
- Do not use text-SR metrics to judge natural texture or face categories.
